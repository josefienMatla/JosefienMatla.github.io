Tippie and Toppie

The Project Overview

This project has been carried out in cooperation with Triple and is presented at the University of Applied Sciences as concluding Thesis for my bachelor. The delivered application is used by minors, children of age six till n in order to tackle language deficiencies. The project has been approached by conducting literature research, field research and brainstorm sessions. The application has been tested several times for validation.

Role

Designer
Concept Creator
Director

Deliverables

Prototype
Design Rationale
Product Biography

The Story

Tippie and Toppie are two good friends from planet X. Tippie has trouble reading and is therefore sent off to the planet islands to develop his reading skills. However, Tippie cannot do this on his own, he needs help from Toppie and the minor who is using the application. During the adventure the three of them go through they come across various assignments they have to solve by reading.

A Problem and Solution

The problem is that many children in the Netherlands leave primary school with a language deficiency. Moreover, the popularity of reading among this group is declining. The goal of this concept and application is to increase enthusiasm for reading and develop reading skills among minors.

Research and conducted experiments have shown the importance of multisensory learning, learning by means of seeing, hearing, feeling and reproduction combined. The application should be used in combination with a physical pawn. The adventures throughout the story in the application have to be tackled using this pawn.

This project has been carried out with focus on the iterative process.
